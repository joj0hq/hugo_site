A language learning call app that connects you with native speakers of your generation. Offering a new language learning experience with real-time calls, AI feedback, and personalized learning.

### Key Features

**1. Real-time Calls**
Improve your language skills while enjoying conversations with people from around the world through high-quality voice calls. Connect with native speakers of your generation and learn living expressions through actual conversations.

**2. AI Feedback**
After conversations, AI checks your pronunciation and grammar, efficiently improving your language skills. Receive personalized lessons tailored to your level and interests.

**3. Personalized Learning**
Receive customized lessons based on your interests and level. Build confidence in speaking through the AI Report → Lesson → Practice cycle.

## Triple (formerly micce)

<div style="background-color: #f5f5f5; padding: 0.5em 1em; border-radius: 6px; margin-bottom: 1rem; display: inline-block;">
<span style="color: #d32f2f; font-weight: 600;">⚠️ Service Discontinued</span>

![Triple](/images/micce_travel.png)

<p style="margin: 1rem 0 1.5rem 0; line-height: 1.7;">
A travel support app where you can complete everything from creating travel plans to making reservations in a single app.
</p>

### Key Features

**1. All Information About Your Destination in One Place**
Information from services used for place selection, such as Google Maps ratings important for choosing shops and hotels, and TikTok short videos showing the atmosphere of places, are consolidated. No need for the hassle of researching across multiple services, which makes travel planning cumbersome.

**2. Easy Hotel Booking**
Integration with major hotel booking services allows you to safely make reservations through the service you usually use.

**3. Many Convenient Features for Creating and Editing Travel Plans**
Rich features support optimal routes and travel times. Even those unfamiliar with the travel destination can create travel plans with confidence.
